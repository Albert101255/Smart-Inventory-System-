Algorithm Methodology

Our Stockr-Predict™ engine analyzes 60 days of historical stock-out events to calculate a rolling Average Daily Consumption rate. The stockout date is projected by applying this rate against current physical inventory levels. Critical alerts are triggered when depletion is expected within 7 days.
